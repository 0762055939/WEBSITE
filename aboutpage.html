ABOUT US

Vereeniging Arts Society was founded in 2020, where it is dedicated to creating a vibrant arts community by supporting local artists and fostering creactivity.

Our Mission

We aim to foster creativity and cultural expression within South African communities by providing accessible art education, workshops, and exhibitions that empower individuals and groups to express themselves artistically, celebrate diversity, and preserve cultural heritage.
